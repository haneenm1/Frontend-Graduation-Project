About Us

Sky Medical was established in Dubai in 2013, providing solutions in medical devices and hearing aids. Another branch was opened in Abu Dhabi in 2018.

The idea of establishing Sky Hearing and Speech Center in Palestine came to deliver the best hearing aids and services nationwide, with skilled specialists using advanced tools and tests.

Hear the world clearly, every day.

Audiology Department

This department provides comprehensive hearing tests for all age groups:

  • Middle Ear Examination - Tympanometry
  • Pure Tone Audiometry Test
  • Hearing Screening for Children - OAE
  • Brainstem Auditory Evoked Response - ABR

We fit advanced German hearing aids (Hansaton) and provide full maintenance and accessories.

Giving voice to every story.

Speech & Language Therapy

Individual treatment plans and weekly follow-ups ensure progress and guidance.
