Welcome to the Hearing Department

About the Hearing Department

Our Hearing Department is equipped with the latest technology and staffed by experienced audiologists. We specialize in comprehensive hearing assessments, hearing aid fittings, and rehabilitation services to improve your auditory health and quality of life.

Why This Appointment Form?

Booking an appointment ensures you receive personalized care tailored to your hearing needs. This form helps us schedule your visit efficiently, prepare the necessary equipment, and allocate enough time for a thorough examination and consultation.

How Do We Perform Hearing Tests?

Our hearing tests involve a series of non-invasive and painless procedures, including pure tone audiometry, speech audiometry, and tympanometry. These tests help us understand your hearing profile and determine if you need a hearing aid or other interventions.

Hearing Aid Fitting Process

After assessment, if you require hearing aids, we guide you through selecting the right device and customize the settings for your comfort and optimal performance. We also provide follow-up support and maintenance services to ensure your satisfaction.
