Our Services

Therapeutic consultations

The center provides specialized therapeutic consultations in hearing and speech problems, to help patients develop communication skills and social integration

Hearint test

Accurate and comprehensive examination services for hearing problems, in addition to customized treatment to help improve the quality of daily life

Comprehensive support

Integrated support in physical therapy and occupational therapy to improve patients' movement and ability to perform daily activities independently.

Specialized treatment

ATreatment services tailored to each individual's needs, to ensure they receive the best healthcare according to their personal circumstances.

Continuous follow-up

Providing continuous follow-up sessions to ensure the improvement of health status with the necessary adjustments to the treatment plan.

Flexible treatment plans

Providing flexible treatment plans that can be modified based on the progress of the case and the changing needs of patients.
